Tariffs for services related to vehicles


Tariffs for services in the field of registration and accounting for vehicles


  • Identification of a vehicle using the VIN number: free of charge
  • Obtaining a vehicle passport (PTS): € 1,500.00
  • Obtaining an insurance policy for a vehicle: € 3 000.00 upon registration, € 500.00 monthly
  • Obtaining number plates: free of charge when obtaining state-issued number plates; € 2,000.00 when receiving custom number plates

Fees for changing data in state registration for vehicles


  • Editing data in the vehicle registration document (PTS): € 500.00
  • Editing data in the vehicle insurance policy: € 500.00
  • Reissue of number plates: € 500,00
